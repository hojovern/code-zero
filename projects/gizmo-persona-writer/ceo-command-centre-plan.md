# CEO Command Centre: Curriculum Outline
**Instructor: Dex**

## Phase 1: The Foundation (Week 1)
1. **The Setup Quest**: Installing the core tools (Node.js, Supabase CLI, and the Terminal).
2. **First Signal**: Connecting your local environment to a live Postgres database.
3. **The Brain Link**: Setting up your first Vector Store for long-term memory.

## Phase 2: Building Intelligence (Week 2)
4. **Assume Persona**: Architecting the "Staff" logic (How to define a Voice Fingerprint).
5. **Gizmosis Loop**: Implementing your first recursive crawler to ingest business context.
6. **Grounding Facts**: Using RAG to make your AI stop hallucinating.

## Phase 3: The Dashboard (Week 3)
7. **Command UI**: Building the SvelteKit Command Centre dashboard.
8. **Real-time Pulse**: Connecting Binky (Images) and Gizmo (Text) into one view.
9. **Action Buttons**: Triggering background agents from a single click.

## Phase 4: Automation & Scale (Week 4)
10. **The Auto-Scheduler**: Setting up Cron jobs for your AI staff.
11. **Autonomous Bridge**: Automating the Blog -> X -> Instagram pipeline.
12. **The Launchpad**: Deploying your own Command Centre to the world. GG!
