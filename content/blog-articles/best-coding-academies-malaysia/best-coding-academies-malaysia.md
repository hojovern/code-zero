## 4. 42 Kuala Lumpur — Best Free Option

**Rating:** Highly respected globally
**Duration:** Self-paced (typically 2–3 years for full program)
**Cost:** Completely FREE
**Focus:** Peer-to-peer learning, computer science fundamentals
**Best For:** Self-motivated learners who thrive without structure

### Overview

42KL is part of a global network spanning 54 cities. The catch? No teachers, no lectures, no tuition—just you and your peers solving increasingly complex problems.

**How it works:**
- 26-day "Piscine" (intensive trial bootcamp)
- Peer-to-peer learning model
- Project-based progression
- No prerequisites—60% start with zero coding knowledge

**Pros:**
- Completely free tuition
- Accessible: 40% of students from B40 backgrounds
- Builds strong problem-solving skills
- Global network and recognition

**Cons:**
- Extremely self-directed (not for everyone)
- Takes 2–3 years for full program
- No job guarantee or placement services
- Less focused on modern web development

**Best for:** Self-starters who want free education and don't need structure.
