# code:zero Penang Venue Research: Taster Sessions

This document outlines the top recommended venues for the 3-hour "AI Architect Initiation" sessions in George Town, Penang.

---

## 1. Hundred Years (Muntri Street)
*   **Vibe:** Premium restored pre-war heritage building.
*   **Suitability:** Best for Brand Alignment. Matches the RM 14,800 course price point.
*   **Key Features:** Unlimited high-speed internet, dedicated meeting venues, exclusive atmosphere.
*   **Action:** Visit hundredyears.space.

## 2. INFINITY8 Reserve (Beach Street)
*   **Vibe:** Luxury Business / White Glove.
*   **Suitability:** Best for corporate-leaning CEOs or those requiring guaranteed high-end service.
*   **Key Features:** Housed in a 100-year-old UNESCO heritage building, high-speed Wi-Fi, premium meeting rooms.
*   **Action:** Visit infinity8.com.my.

## 3. Settlements (Near Penang Road / Chulia)
*   **Vibe:** Modern Industrial / Startup Lab.
*   **Suitability:** Best for "Hacker" energy and high-performance tech workshops.
*   **Key Features:** Proven track record for tech events, reliable fiber internet, central location.
*   **Action:** Visit settlements.work.

## 4. narrow marquee (Malay Street)
*   **Vibe:** Authentic Heritage Wildcard.
*   **Suitability:** Best for a "Gritty Builder" vibe on Malay Street.
*   **Key Features:** Boutique event space, highly localized Georgetown feel.
*   **Action:** Physical visit required (Lebuh Melayu).

---

## Strategic Scouting Tips
*   **Fiber Audit:** Confirm < 20ms ping to AI endpoints.
*   **Power Load:** Ensure capacity for 8+ MacBooks + AC.
*   **Restrooms:** High standard required for CEO satisfaction.
*   **Parking:** Identify nearby lots for busy executives.
