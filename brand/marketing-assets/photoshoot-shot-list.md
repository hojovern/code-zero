# code:zero Staged Photoshoot: Shot List
**Location:** Hundred Years, Muntri St.
**Vibe:** Professional, High-Focus, Heritage meets Future.

---

## 1. The "Architect" (The Hero)
*   **The Shot:** You (JV) standing at the head of the table. Projector is on, showing a "Code Architecture" diagram. 6 students are looking at you, one is nodding.
*   **Purpose:** Website Hero Section. Establishes Authority.

## 2. The "Command Center" (The Tech)
*   **The Shot:** A tight, low-angle shot of a MacBook Pro next to a coffee cup. The screen shows a Streamlit dashboard with "CEO AI" branding. In the blurred background, people are working.
*   **Purpose:** Background for "The Stack" section.

## 3. The "Breakthrough" (The Emotion)
*   **The Shot:** Two people (friends) sitting side-by-side. One is pointing at the other's screen. Both look genuinely excited/surprised.
*   **Purpose:** Social Proof / Testimonials section. Shows the "Aha!" moment.

## 4. The "Heritage Lab" (The Space)
*   **The Shot:** Wide shot of the whole room. Capture the contrast of the 100-year-old walls/windows with the modern laptops and blue glow of the projector.
*   **Purpose:** "The Campus" section. Justifies the premium price point.

## 5. The "Networking" (The Community)
*   **The Shot:** Group standing by the window or at the coffee station. Relaxed, laughing, but still holding MacBooks or notebooks. 
*   **Purpose:** Shows that this isn't just a course, it's a network of peers.

---

## 6. Video Snippet (Optional but Recommended)
*   **The Shot:** 10 seconds of you typing fast, then looking up at the projector and saying: "And that's how you automate the entire reporting chain." (Followed by a quick pan to the students' impressed faces).
*   **Purpose:** Instagram/LinkedIn ad creative.
